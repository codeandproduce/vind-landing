My Projects

Hackathon Projects

Milky (WebHacks2017 Project: 1st Place)

Milky is an app that helps the user organize tasks and milestones in achieving goals. This was our entry to WebHacks2017 where we won 1st place in. We spent 6 days designing and developing this app. The company that sponsored this hackathon wrote about us here.

Amusebrain (MHacks2017 Project)

AMuseBrain was our project for MHacks2017. We integrated EEGs with the platform and the webpage would produce analysis of four brain waves such as anxiety levels, attention levels, and more. We also integrated a feature that detects whether a user blinked using analysis of brain waves to authenticate users.

Open Dictionary (TechCrunch Shanghai Hackathon 2018 Project)

Open Dictionary was our entry to the TechCrunch Hackathon 2018. This is a platform like Wikipedia but for words & definitions. People are able to request translations, add their own translations, and query the dictionary.

More Projects

ALI-OSS-GZIP Node Module on NPM

In my internship at a company, they wanted me to create a NodeJS module that periodically compresses their backup and uploads them to the ALI-OSS platform. This module greatly simplifies the process and has received almost 550 downloads.

Helpline Project Magnifi

Project Magnifi is an NGO founded by Michelle Sun created for those that suffer from Body Dysmorphia. She wanted a way for people to privately converse with her and I created this chatroom entirely from scratch using SocketIO, MongoDB, Node.js.

Ornament Website

CID Organizer
